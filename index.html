Our Vision

EcoGrow Cameroon is a social venture tackling critical waste management challenges in southwest Cameroon. We transform collected waste into high-quality, eco-friendly fertilizers distributed to farmers at subsidized prices. Our initiative supports sustainable farming, creates green jobs, and promotes a cleaner environment for the community.

Innovation in Waste Management

We implement a robust, community-based collection system that diverts tons of organic waste from landfills every week.

Nourishing the Land

Our final product is a nutrient-dense, natural fertilizer that regenerates soil health and boosts the yields of local farms.

Empowering the Community

We create dignified, stable employment opportunities, particularly for women and youth, fueling local economic growth and skill development.

What We Do

High-Quality Fertilizer Production

Organic waste is meticulously composted and converted into nutrient-rich, affordable fertilizers to boost crop yields sustainably.

Empowering Smallholder Farmers

We supply subsidized fertilizers to local farmers, lowering their input costs, increasing profitability, and improving regional food security.
